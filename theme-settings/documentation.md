## 1. Typography & Fonts

Under the **Typography** section in Theme Settings, merchants can configure global body and heading fonts.

- **Body Font**: Controls the font family used for body paragraphs, description text, navigation menus, and form labels.
- **Heading Font**: Controls the font family used for product titles, section headers, and banners.
- **Custom Fonts Integration**: The theme is configured to load and support custom premium fonts such as **Canela Text** and standard Google Fonts like **Inter** and **Cairo** (optimized for Arabic RTL locales).

---

## 2. Font Sizes

To ensure optimal readability across mobile, tablet, and desktop screens, the theme provides granular control over font sizes:

- **Small Text (10px - 16px)**: Affects product badges, category labels, and small metadata.
- **Body Small (11px - 18px)**: Controls text for secondary prices, metadata, and product card titles.
- **Base Body Text (12px - 20px)**: The default font size for main descriptions, articles, and general text.
- **Medium Heading (14px - 28px)**: Controls block-level headings and standard call-to-actions.
- **Large Heading (18px - 48px)**: Sets the scale for homepage banner titles and section headers.

---

## 3. Layout & Grid Settings

Allows merchants to control site-wide containment and responsive element spacing:

- **Page Width (1000px - 1600px)**: Sets the maximum container width for sections, ensuring proper visual alignment on wide desktop monitors.
- **Section Spacing (0px - 100px)**: Adds customizable margins between template blocks to create clean breathing space or compact landing pages.
- **Grid Horizontal & Vertical Space (0px - 100px)**: Controls column and row margins inside product collections, brand listings, and search results.

---

## 4. Corner Roundness

Adjusts border-radius values globally across key user interface components:

- **Card Corner Radius (0px - 32px)**: Applies to product grids, promotion banners, and content boxes.
- **Large Element Corner Radius (0px - 48px)**: Applies to massive layout blocks, popups, drawers, and hero sections.
- **Button Corner Radius (0px - 50px)**: Sets the border-radius across standard interactive triggers.

---

## 5. Animations & Effects

Enlivens the user experience with modern transitions:

- **Reveal Sections on Scroll**: Enables/disables an Intersection Observer that fades and slides sections upward smoothly as the user scrolls.
- **Hover Effect**:
  - `None`: Disables special interaction transforms.
  - `Vertical Lift`: Slightly lifts cards, brands, and buttons upward on hover.
  - `3D Lift`: Scale-lifts cards and buttons with a sleek shadow effect for premium modern appeal.

---

## 6. Buttons Customizer Settings

Allows complete style configuration of theme buttons:

- **Border Controls**:
  - **Thickness (0px - 12px)**: Outer stroke width.
  - **Opacity (0% - 100%)**: Visual strength of the border stroke.
  - **Corner Radius (0px - 50px)**: Button roundness.
- **Shadow Controls**:
  - **Opacity (0% - 100%)**: Intensity of the drop shadow.
  - **Horizontal Offset (-20px - 20px)**: Left/right direction of shadow.
  - **Vertical Offset (-20px - 20px)**: Down/up offset of shadow.
  - **Blur (0px - 40px)**: Softness of the shadow edges.

---

## 7. Variant Pills Customizer Settings

Configures the product variant size/color swatches displayed on product detail pages and quick shop templates:

- **Border Controls**:
  - **Thickness (0px - 12px)**: Stroke width of variant selectors.
  - **Opacity (0% - 100%)**: Contrast level of pill borders.
  - **Corner Radius (0px - 50px)**: Shape configuration (e.g. `0px` for rectangles, `40px+` for pills).
- **Shadow Controls**:
  - **Opacity, Offset, and Blur**: Fully customizable parameters to project elevation/depth behind variant option pill options.

---

## 8. Inputs Customizer Settings

Configures styles for form inputs, text areas, selection fields, and search boxes across the entire theme:

- **Border Controls**:
  - **Thickness (0px - 12px)**: Stroke thickness of input fields.
  - **Opacity (0% - 100%)**: Visual transparency of input borders.
  - **Corner Radius (0px - 50px)**: Curvature of the input edges.
- **Shadow Controls**:
  - **Opacity, Offset, and Blur**: Fully customizable values to create flat or elevated modern drop shadow layouts on input selectors.

---

## 9. Product Cards Customizer Settings

Fine-tune design properties of the product grid catalog cards:

- **Border Controls**:
  - **Thickness (0px - 12px)**: Border thickness of product cards.
  - **Opacity (0% - 100%)**: Contrast of card outline borders.
  - **Corner Radius (0px - 50px)**: Roundness of the product card outer container.
- **Shadow Controls**:
  - **Opacity, Offset, and Blur**: Customizable parameters to draw premium depth and shadows behind product grid showcase elements.

---

## 10. Collection Cards Customizer Settings

Adjust visual parameters for category preview blocks on collection list directories:

- **Border Controls**:
  - **Thickness (0px - 12px)**: Outline stroke width.
  - **Opacity (0% - 100%)**: Opacity of the card borders.
  - **Corner Radius (0px - 50px)**: Edge curvature of collection links.
- **Shadow Controls**:
  - **Opacity, Offset, and Blur**: Control drop shadows and elevation behind collection cards.

---

## 11. Section-Level Design Overrides

While settings are globally configured in **Theme Settings**, sections (such as Header, Footer, and Above Footer Banner) allow local design overrides (e.g. customized logo text alignment, custom menus, text/image combinations, and brand sizing offsets) to achieve tailored custom layouts on specific templates.
